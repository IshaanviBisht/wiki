All types of numbers in math

🔢 1. Natural Numbers (N)

Definition: Counting numbers starting from 1.

Example: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, ...

🔁 2. Whole Numbers (W)

Definition: Natural numbers plus zero.

Example: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, ...

➖ 3. Integers (Z)

Definition: All positive and negative whole numbers, including zero.

Example: -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3, ...

➗ 4. Rational Numbers (Q)

Definition: Numbers that can be written as a fraction (p/q), where q ≠ 0.

Example: 1/2, -3/4, 5, 0.75

🔄 5. Irrational Numbers

Definition: Numbers that cannot be written as a fraction. Their decimal form goes on forever without repeating.

Example: √2, π (pi), e

🔣 6. Real Numbers (R)

Definition: All rational and irrational numbers together.

Example: -5, 0, 1.5, π

🧙 7. Imaginary Numbers

Definition: Numbers that involve i, where i = √-1 (not real).

Example: i, 2i, -5i

🧠 8. Complex Numbers

Definition: Combination of real and imaginary numbers.

Example: 3 + 2i, -1 - 4i
